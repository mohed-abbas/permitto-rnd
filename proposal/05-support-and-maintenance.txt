================================================================================
5.2 HYPERCARE PERIOD (Weeks 1-4 After Go-Live)
================================================================================

  Dedicated intensive support immediately after production deployment to
  ensure smooth adoption.

  Aspect           | Detail
  -----------------|--------------------------------------------------------
  Duration         | 4 weeks from go-live date
  Availability     | Sunday-Thursday, business hours (or as agreed)
  Response Time    | Critical issues: within 2 hours. Other issues: within
                   | 4 hours
  Monitoring       | Daily system health checks - server, database,
                   | background jobs, email delivery
  Scope            | Bug fixes, performance issues, workflow adjustments,
                   | user support, configuration changes
  Communication    | Direct channel (WhatsApp/Teams/email) with the
                   | development team


================================================================================
5.3 ACTIVE SUPPORT & MAINTENANCE (Ongoing)
================================================================================

  Continued support after the hypercare period to keep the system running
  smoothly, secure, and up to date.


  WHAT'S INCLUDED:

  Category                     | Coverage
  -----------------------------|--------------------------------------------
  Bug Fixes                    | Identification and resolution of software
                               | defects - no additional charge for bugs in
                               | delivered features
  Security Updates             | Regular security patches for dependencies,
                               | frameworks, and server software
  Server Monitoring            | Monitoring of application health, database
                               | performance, background job execution, and
                               | disk usage
  Database Maintenance         | Routine database optimization, backup
                               | verification, and performance tuning
  Uptime Monitoring            | System availability monitoring with alerts
                               | for downtime
  Backup Management            | Automated daily database backups with
                               | verification
  SSL Certificate Management   | SSL/TLS certificate renewal and
                               | configuration
  Email Service Monitoring     | Monitoring email delivery success rates and
                               | troubleshooting delivery issues
  Background Job Monitoring    | Ensuring scheduled jobs (daily reminders,
                               | suspension checks, expiry warnings) execute
                               | reliably
  Infrastructure Management     | VPS server, domain, and email service
                               | (Resend) - provisioned, managed, and
                               | renewed by us. Recurring costs included
                               | in the monthly maintenance fee
  Minor Adjustments            | Small configuration changes, wording
                               | updates, notification timing adjustments


  SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA):

  Priority  | Description                  | Response   | Resolution
            |                              | Time       | Target
  ----------|------------------------------|------------|------------------
  Critical  | System down, no users can    | Within     | Within 8 hours
            | access, data loss risk       | 2 hours    |
  High      | Major feature broken         | Within     | Within 24 hours
            | (approvals not working, PDFs | 4 hours    |
            | not generating, notifications|            |
            | failing)                     |            |
  Medium    | Feature partially impaired,  | Within     | Within 3
            | workaround available         | 8 hours    | business days
  Low       | Minor UI issue, cosmetic     | Within     | Within 5
            | defect, non-urgent request   | 24 hours   | business days


  SUPPORT AVAILABILITY:

  Aspect                  | Detail
  ------------------------|--------------------------------------------------
  Support Hours           | Sunday-Thursday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (client's
                          | timezone)
  Emergency Support       | Critical issues (system down) - available outside
                          | business hours via emergency contact
  Communication Channels  | Dedicated support channel (WhatsApp/Teams/email -
                          | as agreed with client)
  Issue Reporting         | Client reports issues via agreed channel with
                          | description and screenshots

================================================================================
5.5 BACKUP & DISASTER RECOVERY
================================================================================

  Aspect                       | Detail
  -----------------------------|---------------------------------------------
  Database Backups             | Automated daily backups with 30-day retention
  Backup Storage               | Stored separately from production server for
                               | redundancy
  Recovery Time Objective      | System restored within 4 hours of a critical
  (RTO)                        | failure
  Recovery Point Objective     | Maximum 24 hours of data loss (from last
  (RPO)                        | daily backup)
  Disaster Recovery Test       | Backup restoration tested quarterly to verify
                               | integrity
